Frequently asked questions

Do I book directly through this page?

No. Small business filming is arranged on an ad hoc basis depending on where we are, how our schedule is tracking, and what can reasonably be accommodated at the time. To discuss a suitable day and time, please send us an enquiry through this page or email us directly at showcasingoztv@gmail.com. You are also welcome to view our Calendar page to see when we are expected to be in your region.

What types of businesses are suitable?

These packages suit retailers, cafés, service providers, makers, studios, trades, wellness businesses, tourism-linked businesses, and other regional operators along our filming route.

Can staff be involved in the filming?

Yes. Staff involvement often strengthens the storytelling and helps customers connect more personally with your business.

What if operations are busy on the day?

We work with you to capture the strongest possible content while minimising disruption to your customers and normal operations.

Want to secure a suitable filming time?

Filming availability in each town is limited. To view available appointment times while we are in your region, visit our Calendar page. If you have questions first, send an enquiry below.
