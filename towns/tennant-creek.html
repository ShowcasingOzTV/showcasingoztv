Warumungu

Warumungu refers to the traditional owners of the Tennant Creek region, whose Country encompasses much of the Barkly area. The Warumungu people maintain strong cultural connections to the land, with sites of spiritual, historical, and cultural significance throughout the region.

Devils Marbles

Devils Marbles, known as Karlu Karlu, is a world-renowned geological site featuring massive rounded granite boulders scattered across the landscape. The area is of great cultural significance to local Aboriginal communities and is one of the Northern Territory‚Äôs most iconic natural landmarks.
