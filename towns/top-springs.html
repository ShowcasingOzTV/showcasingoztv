Top Springs (NT)

Top Springs is a tiny yet iconic outback stop located at the junction of the **Buchanan Highway** and the **Buntine Highway**, deep in the Northern Territory‚Äôs Victoria River region. Surrounded by wide-open plains, cattle country and rugged NT landscapes, it serves as an important rest point for travellers navigating remote roads.

The Top Springs Hotel is the heart of this small locality, offering fuel, food, accommodation and classic Territory hospitality. Adventurers travelling through the Tanami, Gregory National Park region or heading toward Katherine often rely on Top Springs as a crucial break in their journey.

ShowcasingOz TV will be capturing the roadhouse atmosphere, desert landscapes and unique outback character that make Top Springs a memorable stop in remote NT.
