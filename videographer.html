Videographer / Video Editor (Contract)

We are seeking an experienced Videographer / Video Editor to join ShowcasingOz TV for the 2026 travel season. You'll work alongside our host, capturing beautiful stories of Australian towns, tourism providers, and regional experiences.

Requirements

  • Strong experience in videography + editing
  • Own professional camera & audio equipment
  • Ability to travel for extended periods
  • Strong storytelling and pacing skills in post-production

Contract

  • Weekly contractor payment
  • Travel + accommodation included
