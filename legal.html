Legal Information

This page outlines the legal policies, filming guidelines and website terms for ShowcasingOz TV. These policies help ensure responsible filmmaking, transparency, and protection for attractions, communities and website users.

1. Filming Ethics & Animal Welfare Policy

ShowcasingOz TV is committed to ethical, respectful filmmaking. Our mission includes promoting Australia's tourism, culture and diversity through responsible storytelling while protecting wildlife and respecting local communities.

Animal Welfare Commitment

  • No handling or close interaction with wildlife unless authorised and supervised.
  • No entering restricted, rehabilitation or staff-only areas.
  • No flash photography or disruptive lighting around animals.
  • No behaviour that may stress or disturb animals.
  • We fully respect attractions that decline filming for animal welfare reasons.

Respect for Attraction Policies

Attractions have full authority over whether filming is permitted. When filming is declined:

  • No filming occurs on-site.
  • No staff or visitors are recorded.
  • The attraction may still be mentioned verbally for regional accuracy.

Cultural & Indigenous Respect

  • We follow all guidance from Traditional Owners and authorised Elders.
  • No filming of sacred sites, ceremonies or restricted artwork without permission.
  • We commit to culturally safe and accurate storytelling.

Minimal Impact Filming

We use compact, low-impact equipment and avoid disrupting walkways, tours or guest experiences.

Safety & Accuracy

We prioritise safety for wildlife, staff, visitors and our crew. Operators may request factual corrections where appropriate.

2. Filming & Permissions Disclaimer

Permission to Film

Filming inside attractions, sanctuaries, accommodation providers or private locations only occurs when explicit permission is granted. If permission is denied, no footage is captured.

Public Space Filming

Filming in public locations complies with Australian laws and local council requirements.

Copyright & Ownership

All footage filmed by ShowcasingOz TV is exclusively owned by ShowcasingOz TV unless otherwise agreed.

Drone Use

Drones are operated only when allowed under CASA laws and attraction/operator policies.

Withdrawal of Permission

Operators may withdraw permission at any time. Footage will be altered or removed if required.
