Legal Information

This page outlines the full legal, ethical and operational policies governing ShowcasingOz TV, a tourism media organisation owned and operated by David Stidston (ABN 26 731 217 803), registered in Tasmania, Australia.

1. Filming Ethics & Animal Welfare Policy

ShowcasingOz TV conducts all filming with respect, care and professionalism to protect wildlife, guests, staff, cultural sensitivities and the natural environment.

Wildlife & Animal Welfare

  • No handling of wildlife unless explicitly approved and supervised.
  • No filming in rehabilitation or restricted zones.
  • No drones near wildlife or nesting areas unless permitted.
  • No actions that disrupt, stress or alter animal behaviour.

Cultural & Indigenous Respect

  • No filming of sacred sites, artwork or ceremonies without permission.
  • We follow guidance from Traditional Owners and Elders.
  • Cultural protocols override filming desires at all times.

Environmental Responsibility

  • Leave-no-trace principles apply at all filming locations.
  • No disturbance of natural habitats.
  • Compliance with National Parks, Council and Ranger instructions.

2. Drone Operations Policy

ShowcasingOz TV adheres strictly to CASA (Civil Aviation Safety Authority) regulations.

  • No drone flights in prohibited or wildlife-sensitive zones.
  • No flying over private property without consent.
  • No drone use in strong winds, populated areas or near airports.

3. Filming Permissions Disclaimer

Filming inside attractions, sanctuaries, accommodations or businesses only occurs when explicit permission is granted.

  • If an operator declines filming, no footage is captured on-site.
  • Only general verbal references may be included for accuracy.
  • We comply immediately with any withdrawal of permission.

4. Media Collaboration Terms

  • Participation in any package does not guarantee final editorial inclusion.
  • Operators may not request specific portrayal style.
  • Provided perks (e.g., stays, tickets) do not constitute paid sponsorship unless agreed in writing.
  • ShowcasingOz TV retains creative control.

5. Intellectual Property & Licensing

  • All filmed content is owned exclusively by ShowcasingOz TV.
  • Operators may request usage rights separately, subject to approval.
  • No unauthorised reuse, editing or redistribution of our footage.
  • Supplied media from operators is deemed licensed for editorial use.

6. Content Removal / Takedown Policy

Operators may submit a removal request if:

  • Filming breached communicated policies.
  • Footage reveals restricted areas or private individuals.
  • There is cultural or safety-related concern.

All requests are reviewed within 14 days.

7. Travel Information Accuracy Disclaimer

Information about towns, attractions, accommodations and businesses is accurate at the time of writing. However, opening hours, prices, facilities and availability may change at any time.

8. Privacy Policy

  • We collect names, emails and messages submitted through forms.
  • Analytics data is collected anonymously.
  • No information is sold or shared, except where legally required.

9. Accessibility Statement

ShowcasingOz TV aims to provide an accessible website experience. We work to ensure compatibility with mobile devices, screen readers and modern browsers.

10. Liability Disclaimer

ShowcasingOz TV is not responsible for:

  • Travel decisions made based on our content.
  • Changes in operator availability, pricing or conditions.
  • Visitor safety at attractions or locations featured.

11. Talent Release Summary

All presenters, guests and participants appearing in our footage sign appropriate release forms authorising usage in our series and promotional materials.

12. Governing Law

This website, content and agreements are governed by the laws of **Tasmania, Australia**.
