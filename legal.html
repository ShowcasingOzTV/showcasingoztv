1. Filming Ethics & Animal Welfare Policy

ShowcasingOz TV is committed to responsible, respectful and ethical filmmaking. We avoid impacting wildlife, guests or operations during filming and always follow attraction policies.

Animal Welfare

  • No handling or close interactions unless approved.
  • No filming in restricted or rehabilitation areas.
  • No disruptive lighting or behaviour.

Attraction Policies

  • If filming permission is declined, no on-site filming occurs.

Cultural Respect

  • No filming of sacred or culturally restricted sites.
  • We follow guidance from Traditional Owners and Elders.
