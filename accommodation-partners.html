Accommodation Marketing & Filming Packages

ShowcasingOz TV is filming across Australia from June–December 2026. If your accommodation is on our route, we can showcase your property and supply clear marketing assets designed to increase visibility, strengthen booking confidence, and help convert travellers into enquiries and bookings.

How you can use your content

All videos and images produced through ShowcasingOz TV are delivered ready for practical marketing use. This means your property receives content that can continue working for you long after filming day.

🌐 Website & Booking Pages

Use your video and imagery on your homepage, room pages, accommodation pages, and direct booking pages.

📱 Social Media & Advertising

Use short clips and still images for reels, boosted posts, ongoing social campaigns, and paid ads.

🛎️ OTA & Listing Profiles

Support your presence across Google Business, tourism listings, booking platforms, and other online directories.

📧 Email & Trade Promotion

Strengthen email campaigns, visitor information promotions, travel trade presentations, and partnership marketing.

Why accommodation owners choose this

Increase booking confidence

Travellers often book accommodation based on visuals. Video helps guests understand the property before they arrive.

Show more than photos

Video allows potential guests to experience the layout, atmosphere, and surroundings of your property.

Stand out from competitors

Many properties rely on outdated or limited images. Professional visual content helps your accommodation stand out online.

Reusable marketing assets

Your business receives content that can be reused across multiple campaigns rather than being limited to one release.
