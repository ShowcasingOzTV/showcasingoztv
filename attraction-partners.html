Attraction Partnerships

ShowcasingOz TV partners with tourism attractions across Australia to create engaging, presenter-led stories that drive visitation and brand awareness.

Our Attraction Services

These services are available across all packages depending on the level selected.

T1 — Base Filmed Attraction Feature

What We Do
  • We film a professionally shot feature of your attraction.
What You Receive
  • Inclusion in the relevant episode featuring your town/region.

T2 — Presenter‑Led Walk‑Through

What We Do
  • Our presenter guides viewers through your attraction, highlighting key experiences.
What You Receive
  • A more engaging and personalised segment, increasing viewer connection.

T3 — Standalone Cut of Your Segment

What We Do
  • We create a separate, clean exported version of your feature.
What You Receive
  • A shareable standalone video for your website, socials or advertising.

T4 — Social Media Cut‑Down Versions

What We Do
  • We produce short edits optimised for social media.
What You Receive
  • 3–5 punchy edits ideal for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and Reels.

T5 — Priority Segment Placement

What We Do
  • We place your attraction earlier in the episode structure.
What You Receive
  • Higher visibility and earlier audience retention impact.

T6 — 30–60 Sec Promotional Ad Creation

What We Do
  • Create a broadcast‑quality mini‑advertisement for your attraction.
What You Receive
  • Your own video ad to use anywhere — online or in‑venue.

T7 — Extended 2–3 Minute Feature

What We Do
  • Film and edit an extended story‑driven segment.
What You Receive
  • A deeper, more immersive feature ideal for campaigns.

T8 — Drone Footage (where permitted)

What We Do
  • Capture aerial shots to elevate your story.
What You Receive
  • Stunning cinematic visuals, included in all relevant edits.

T9 — Promotional Usage Rights

What We Do
  • Provide you with full promotional usage rights.
What You Receive
  • Freedom to use your video(s) across digital and marketing channels.

T10 — Extended Social Promo Bundle

What We Do
  • Create additional micro‑content targeting various demographics.
What You Receive
  • Extra social assets for better reach and campaign flexibility.
