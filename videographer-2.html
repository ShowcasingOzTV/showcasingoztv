About the Role

ShowcasingOz TV is a national Australian travel series highlighting real places, real stories, and authentic tourism experiences across every state and territory. We are now seeking a Videographer / Editor / Co-Presenter to join our travelling Season 1 production team from June to December 2026, helping capture and shape engaging content across regional and outback Australia.

This is a hybrid role suited to someone who is confident behind the camera, capable in the edit, and comfortable stepping into an on-camera support role when required. You will work closely with the founder, presenter, and lead videographer, while also being trusted to film independently at times across separate locations.

What You'll Be Filming

  • Motels, hotels, caravan parks, and unique accommodation stays
  • Local attractions, tours, and tourism operators
  • Scenic landscapes, towns, and regional communities
  • Interviews with owners, operators, and locals
  • Small businesses such as restaurants, cafés, bakeries, mechanics, and other local operators
  • Behind-the-scenes footage and social media content
  • Occasional presenter support segments, walk-throughs, and on-camera contribution where required

At times, filming coverage may be split across different businesses or locations, meaning you may occasionally be required to film solo while the lead videographer captures content elsewhere. This role therefore suits someone who can confidently manage both production quality and professional communication on their own when needed.

🎥 Required Skills & Experience

  • Proven experience as a videographer, content creator, or video editor with a strong portfolio of previous work
  • Ability to operate professional video equipment including cameras, drones, gimbals, microphones, and lighting
  • Experience shooting with professional cinema or mirrorless camera systems
  • Ability to edit fast-turnaround content using professional editing software such as Adobe Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve
  • Experience with colour grading, audio mixing, and exporting for web and broadcast formats
  • Ability to shoot in indoor and outdoor environments, including low-light and fast-paced situations
  • Strong understanding of composition, lighting, audio capture, framing, and visual storytelling
  • Clean and stable shooting style suitable for professional broadcast-quality production
  • Knowledge of file management, backups, and on-location data handling
  • Ability to coordinate effectively with the lead videographer to maintain visual consistency across production
  • Ability to work both independently and as part of a small production team
  • Confidence appearing on camera, speaking naturally, and supporting presentation where required
  • Comfortable with extended travel and filming in regional and remote locations

💼 Professional Requirements

  • Strong attention to detail and consistency
  • Creative thinker who can take direction while also contributing ideas
  • Excellent communication and people skills
  • Professional and presentable when dealing with clients, operators, and members of the public
  • Confidence working one-on-one with business owners and filming professionally without direct supervision at times
  • Comfortable adapting between filming, editing, and occasional on-camera presentation support

🧰 Equipment

We provide core filming gear, and you are welcome to bring additional equipment that you prefer using.

📅 Availability & Workstyle

  • Ability to work long periods on the road, away from home
  • Comfortable with weekends, early starts, and long filming days
  • Strong time-management skills for meeting filming and editing deadlines
  • Ability to adapt to changing locations, schedules, and conditions
  • Willingness to support a flexible production structure across filming, editing, and presentation tasks

What We Provide

  • Accommodation fully covered, with a daily meal allowance allocated
  • Filming schedule and production support
  • Editing equipment access if needed
  • Guaranteed RDO weekly
  • Opportunity to grow into Season 2 and future productions, subject to performance and business growth
  • The chance to be part of a new national Australian travel media platform from the ground up

Who We're Looking For

We’re seeking a positive, reliable, and creative team member who enjoys travelling, meeting people, and helping bring meaningful regional stories to life. You will be someone who can move comfortably between filming, editing, and occasional on-camera support, while working closely with the founder, presenter, and lead videographer to craft engaging episodes and digital content.

🔎 Contract Conditions

This is a contractor role for Season 1 (June–December 2026). Applicants must hold a valid ABN and will be responsible for managing their own tax and superannuation obligations. Subject to performance and business growth, there is potential for the role to continue into Season 2 and beyond.
