Season 1 Vehicle Partner (4WD)

We are seeking a new or demonstrator 4WD (or equivalent vehicle support through a rental partnership) for the Season 1 national filming tour from June–December 2026. In exchange, the vehicle partner becomes our Major Sponsor for Season 1 (valued at $15,000).

Why a Vehicle Partnership Makes Sense

  • Long-duration exposure: the vehicle is featured throughout the Season 1 production period.
  • Highly relevant audience: Australians interested in road trips, regional travel, and 4WD touring.
  • Dealership content assets: we produce branded vehicle content you can use in ads, social posts, and showroom screens.
  • Regional storytelling alignment: your brand is positioned as enabling regional tourism and community visibility.

What the Partner Receives

Brand & Placement

  • Ongoing social media inclusions during tour

Deliverables You Can Use

Activation Options (Choose What Fits)

  • Customer offer integration: optional inclusion of a dealership offer/call-to-action in agreed content (brand-safe).

What We’re Seeking
