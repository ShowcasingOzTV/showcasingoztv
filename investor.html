Investor Opportunity

ShowcasingOz TV is a structured tourism media infrastructure platform producing original long-form and short-form content across regional Australia. Season 1 (June–December 2026) represents the establishment phase of a scalable, multi-season media business designed to build long-term commercial assets.

Project Overview

ShowcasingOz TV operates as a national tourism media platform generating revenue through professionally structured digital marketing packages for tourism operators, regional council media partnerships, sponsorship tiers, and long-term content monetisation.

The business is designed as a repeatable, multi-season operating model. Each filming phase expands the national content library, strengthens commercial relationships, and increases long-term asset leverage.

Capital Raise

ShowcasingOz TV is currently raising:

  • $200,000 in exchange for 35% equity
  • Implied post-money valuation: approximately $571,000

Capital will be deployed into Season 1 production infrastructure, crew and operations, national filming execution, and commercial rollout. This establishes the production base and asset foundation required for scalable operations.

Season Structure

  • Season 1 – Establishment: Infrastructure build, national filming, commercial validation
  • Season 2 – Operational Sustainability: Refined pricing, controlled cost base, margin progression
  • Long-Term Growth: Asset licensing, destination digital products, increased revenue per filming day

Revenue Model

  • Tourism business filming & showcase packages
  • Regional council digital media asset packages
  • Accommodation & attraction partnerships
  • Sponsorship tiers
  • Long-term digital asset monetisation

Revenue is diversified across multiple streams and structured to transition from infrastructure-heavy deployment in Season 1 to margin leverage in subsequent seasons.

Investment Rationale

This opportunity is suited to an investor aligned with tourism, media, or regional development seeking early-stage equity in a structured Australian media platform with defined multi-season growth potential.

ShowcasingOz TV is not a one-off production. Season 1 establishes production capability, commercial proof, and a national content asset base designed to compound in value over time.
