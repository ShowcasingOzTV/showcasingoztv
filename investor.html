Strategic Partnership & Investment

ShowcasingOz TV is preparing to launch a national travel and tourism production across Australia, with Season 1 scheduled to commence on 27 July 2026.

The platform is designed to capture and promote Australia’s destinations, attractions, landmarks, points of interest, accommodation providers, selected small businesses, and regional tourism stories through professionally produced video content distributed to domestic and international audiences.

We are currently finalising strategic partners and investors to support the launch of the first national rollout and establish the foundation for a scalable, multi-season tourism media platform.

Why This Matters

Many regional destinations and tourism operators across Australia remain underexposed, despite having strong visitor appeal and significant local economic value.

ShowcasingOz TV has been created to help bridge that gap by increasing visibility, encouraging visitation, and supporting tourism-related businesses through destination-led storytelling and integrated commercial pathways.

Season 1 Rollout

Season 1 will deliver a structured 20-week filming journey across approximately 40 primary destinations, with broader coverage extending through more than 100 towns and locations.

The rollout is designed to create ongoing exposure for regional destinations while building a long-term content library and commercial platform that can expand across future seasons.

Strategic Partnership & Investment

We are seeking to align with organisations or individuals who understand the value of tourism, regional exposure, travel behaviour, and scalable content distribution.

Opportunities may include strategic investment, commercial partnership integration, category sponsorship, or other aligned support that contributes to the successful launch and growth of ShowcasingOz TV.

Partnership structures are being considered based on alignment, category fit, strategic value, and the level of involvement required.

Use of Funds

Capital and partnership support will be directed toward activating Season 1 and ensuring the production is delivered professionally and on schedule.

  • Professional filming and production equipment
  • Crew engagement and onboarding
  • Pre-production, scheduling, and route coordination
  • Vehicle, fuel, travel, and operational logistics
  • Website, content delivery, and distribution infrastructure
  • Partner integration and commercial rollout activity

A detailed expenses summary and supporting documentation can be provided during discussions with aligned parties.

Revenue Model

  • Partnered filming packages for accommodation providers, attractions, and selected small businesses
  • Destination promotion partnerships with tourism bodies, councils, and regional organisations
  • Integrated sponsorship and brand partnership opportunities
  • Accommodation and travel-related referral pathways
  • Long-term content licensing and digital asset monetisation

The business is structured to generate revenue through multiple channels while expanding the value of its national content library across future seasons.

Timing

We are seeking to confirm final partner and investment alignment within the coming 1 to 2 weeks to allow adequate lead time for crew onboarding, production logistics, route coordination, and partner integration ahead of the scheduled 27 July 2026 commencement.

Next Step

This opportunity is suited to organisations or individuals who see commercial and strategic value in supporting a national tourism platform designed to promote Australian destinations, businesses, and regional communities.

If this aligns with your organisation, interests, or investment focus, we welcome a direct conversation.
