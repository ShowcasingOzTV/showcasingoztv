Sponsorship & Advertising Disclosure

ShowcasingOz TV is committed to transparency in all partnerships, sponsorships, hosted stays, advertising collaborations and commercial relationships. This disclosure explains how we handle paid and unpaid promotional content in accordance with Australian Consumer Law (ACL).

1. Our Commitment to Transparency

We believe audiences deserve to know when content is influenced by a commercial partnership. When ShowcasingOz TV receives payment, hosted accommodation, attraction access, or any other commercial benefit, we disclose this clearly using labels such as:

  • "Filmed in partnership with..."
  • "Sponsored by..."
  • "In collaboration with..."

Disclosures may appear in videos, social media posts, YouTube descriptions, website pages, email communications and printed collateral.

2. Types of Partnerships We Participate In

ShowcasingOz TV engages in several forms of collaborations, including:

  • Paid Sponsorships: Monetary payment for brand visibility or promotional inclusion.
  • Attraction Partnerships: Filming packages purchased by attractions to be featured.
  • Accommodation Partnerships: Hosted stays or discounted stays offered as part of a filming agreement.
  • Tourism Board Collaborations: Support provided by regional or state tourism authorities.
  • Product or Service Sponsorships: Equipment, vehicles, supplies or services provided in exchange for exposure.

3. Editorial Independence

Regardless of sponsorship, ShowcasingOz TV maintains full editorial control over filming, narrative direction, editing and final creative output.

Partners cannot dictate:

  • The storyline or message of the video
  • The opinions expressed by presenters
  • The ranking or comparison of attractions or locations
  • The inclusion or exclusion of competing businesses

Sponsored involvement does not guarantee positive coverage.

4. What We Offer Partners

For transparency, sponsorships and partnerships may include:

  • Featured placement in a video episode
  • On-location filming of attractions, tours, accommodations or events
  • Social media exposure across ShowcasingOz TV channels
  • Mention in supporting articles, blogs or web pages
  • Use of professionally filmed footage for the partnerâ€™s own marketing

5. Hosted Stays & Complimentary Services

When accommodation providers or attractions offer complimentary or discounted services, these are considered a form of sponsorship. We disclose such arrangements where relevant.

6. No Hidden Endorsements

ShowcasingOz TV does not accept payment in exchange for unlabelled endorsements, falsified reviews or misleading representations. All promotional involvement is openly stated.

7. Accuracy & Authenticity

Filming and presenting locations authentically is core to our values. Partners cannot request distortions of:

  • Visitor experience
  • Landscape or environmental conditions
  • Cultural significance
  • Safety considerations

8. Compliance with Australian Consumer Law

We comply with ACL requirements regarding transparency in advertising and sponsorship. Misleading or deceptive conduct is strictly prohibited under section 18 of the ACL.

9. Updates to This Disclosure

This page may be updated periodically as our partnerships evolve or new advertising relationships are introduced.
