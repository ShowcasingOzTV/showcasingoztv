Partner With Us

What “Partnering” Means

Partnering with ShowcasingOz TV doesn’t mean “going into business together”. It simply means your business becomes part of our Season 1 national filming tour — where we professionally film the places, experiences, and local operators that make your town worth stopping for.

We provide professional videography and photography to Accommodation providers, Attractions, and Small Businesses, creating high-quality content that helps travellers discover your region — both domestically and internationally.

The result is a polished showcase that increases visibility, strengthens credibility, and gives you marketing assets you can continue using long after filming is complete.

Limited Filming Availability

ShowcasingOz TV follows a fixed national filming route during Season 1. This means availability within each town and region is limited, and filming opportunities are typically restricted to that filming season only.

Once we move on to the next region, there may not be an opportunity to return in the near future — so if you’re interested in being featured, we recommend enquiring early to secure a place during our scheduled visit.

Small Business Partners

Regional towns are defined by their local businesses. We also film the operators that travellers naturally stop for — helping visitors discover the cafés, eateries, shops, services, and unique experiences that give each town its character.

Examples include:

  • Cafés, coffee shops, bakeries, restaurants, pubs and local eateries
  • Butchers, delis, farm gate producers, breweries, distilleries and wineries
  • Gift shops, boutique retailers, makers, galleries and creative spaces
  • Tour operators, experiences, and unique local businesses
  • Mechanics, tyre shops and automotive services (regional travel essentials)
  • Affordable, structured entry point for regional operators
  • Professional showcase content you can reuse to drive enquiries
  • Increased visibility to travellers passing through your town
